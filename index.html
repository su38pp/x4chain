OUR TECHNOLOGY

1. Efficient and Thorough Distributed System

While existing exchange systems have tried to resolve the issue of inflowing traffic with a hardware scale-out, X4CHAIN’s XChange uses that as a basis for integrating new technologies and constructing the optimal architecture, as well as distributing and assigning roles to each server to effectively store and process data.

2. Introduction of a More Efficient System

We have implemented a unique stock-based matching system that enables a more stable and faster data processing, as well as a memory-based database, a departure from the general database, which allows for a more reliable and faster system.

3. Wallet Service Equipped with a Tight Security

We operate the nodes of all coins independently, and with that, we have further strengthened the security of our Wallet Service by breaking out of the Hot Wallet and Cold Wallet to develop original technologies regarding the management of the Private Key, which is key to wallet configuration.

CEO greeting
